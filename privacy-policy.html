Privacy Policy This extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Data usage: - The extension operates entirely on the user’s device. - User preferences (such as display or filtering settings) are stored locally using browser storage. - No personal information, browsing history, messages, or identifiers are collected or transmitted. Remote resources: - The extension may download static data files (e.g., JSON configuration or blacklist files) from a fixed remote URL. - These files contain no personal data and are used solely to provide the extension’s functionality. - No executable code is downloaded or executed remotely. Third parties: - The extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs. - No data is shared with any third party.
